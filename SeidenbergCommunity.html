The Problem

Students didn't aware of many things which were going on campus such as events, who is their advisor or how is Pace's alumini.

Solution

After meeting Stakeholder and users' we came up with the solution and came up with these 4 main features.

  • News Feed
  • Events -> Tech/Business, Fun Events
  • Calender
  • Chat

User Research

Our users were students. We conducted survey just to know what were students' behavior and their pain points.

    We mainly focused on the following things:

  • Purpose of visit
  • Last Event attended
  • Where they seek out for help if they are in problem

    After conducting user surveys we discoverd the following findings:

  • Students usually come to relax, chat with friends or visit professor in their office hour
  • The were unaware Seidenberg events
  • They usually use front desk for help
  • Unaware of many things such as tutoring, where they can find class rooms, their own schedule
